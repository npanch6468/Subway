Subway algorithm
1.) From classroom, go to nearby door
2.) Go through door
3.) Turn left and walk straight
4.) Take your first right and go to elevator
5.) click on the down arrow elevator button
6.) When elevator door opens, go inside elevator, and press floor one button
7.)	When on first floor, walk out of the elevator
8.) Turn right and go straight until you see a door
9.) go past door
10.)go down the stairs
11.)Go to door
12.)Open door
13.)Go outside and check both sides of road 
14.)Once safe to cross the road, cross the road
15.)Take a left
16.)Walk straight until you see subway
17.)Turn right and go to door
